Short Biography

From January of 2014, i am working with Prof. Danilo Montessi and Matteo Magnani as a postdoctoral researcher at University of Bologna, Italy. Broadly speaking, my research focueses on social networks and distributed systems. Specifically, i am looking at diffusion of information, communities patterns in online social networks.

I completed my Ph.D in school of computer engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore under the supervision of Prof. Anwitaman Datta in 2013. In my thesis titled Decentralized online social networks (DOSN) primitives, I investigated various key primitives which could be helpful in making DOSN successful. In general, my research problem involved to solve family of problems efficiently in a large distributed network by exploring social links, in absence of global knowledge.

I had a mixed of experience from academics and industry before starting my Ph.D. in January 2009. I worked as a lecturer before doing a MS from IIT Roorkee, India, following which I worked with Cisco Systems Bangalore, India for around 2 years.
